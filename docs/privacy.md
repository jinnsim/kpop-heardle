# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-17_

K-Pop Heardle ("the app") is published by Jung Soon Shin. This page
describes what data the app does — and does not — collect.

## Summary

**K-Pop Heardle does not collect any personal data.** Everything you do
in the app stays on your device.

## What stays on your device

- Your daily-puzzle results, streak, and statistics (stored locally
  with Apple's SwiftData framework).
- Your notification and language preferences (stored in iOS
  UserDefaults).
- Any guess input you type (used only in-app for that session; never
  sent anywhere).

## What we fetch from the network

- The daily song catalog and schedule. This is a single static JSON
  file served by jsDelivr (mirroring our GitHub repository). The
  request includes no personal data — only the standard HTTP headers
  your device automatically sends.
- 30-second audio preview clips from Apple's iTunes Search service,
  the same content available to any music app. Apple may log these
  requests under their own policies.
- Optional: the app icon for a cross-promoted sibling app (Biasly),
  fetched once from Apple's iTunes Lookup service.

## What we do NOT collect

- Your name, email, phone number, address.
- Your contacts, photos, calendar, microphone, location, health data.
- Any analytics or telemetry.
- Crash reports beyond what Apple already provides via App Store
  Connect for developers (anonymous, system-wide).
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA). The app does not show ads.

## Third parties

We use Apple's iTunes Search API and Apple Music deep links. Use of
those services is governed by
[Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

The song catalog mirror is served by jsDelivr, a free public CDN.
jsDelivr may log standard HTTP request metadata under their
[privacy policy](https://www.jsdelivr.com/terms/privacy-policy-jsdelivr-net).

## Children

The app is rated 4+ on the App Store and contains no objectionable
content. It does not collect data from children or anyone else.

## Data retention

All on-device data persists until you delete the app or clear its
data via iOS Settings. There is no server-side store to delete from.
